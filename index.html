Dr Ketaki Sharma

Specialist Paediatrician, North Shore Private Hospital

Dr Ketaki Sharma (Ket) is an experienced paediatrician who is committed to listening to caregivers and providing tailored, family-centred support.

Ket is available for all general paediatric referrals, and has special interests in the assessment and management of food allergies, hayfever, eczema and ADHD. She can conduct skin prick testing on site, and can arrange for food challenges and aeroallergen immunotherapy where indicated.
